Welcome on my personal website !

I am PhD student at IPGP in Paris under the supervision of Razvan Caracas.

I am graduated of the bachelor of Earth sciences and the master of physic and theoretical chemistry from the ENS de Lyon.

Research interest

In my PhD project, I use ab initio molecular dynamics on pyrolite and carbonaceous chondrites. I am interested in cosmochemistry, theoretical chemistry, planetary sciences and material sciences. I plan to defend my PhD in September 2025.

In October 2025, I will start a postdoc position at Caltech on the Geological and Planetary Science division fellowship.
